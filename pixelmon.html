    • Pixelmon

    Pixelmon is an NFT game announced back at the start of February 2022, a demo was released on the 7th of February showing some pretty promising future on the game. When the demo was dropped, the development team announced NFT eggs. The premise was that you have to buy one egg and wait for them to hatch. The hatching was scheduled to happen on February 26th 2022 so, when all the NFTs were sold, the team raised a total of 70m$, with each Pixelmon costing at 3 ETH each (10.000$ at the time). When the hatching took place Everyone got disappointed.

    Example of some pixelmons on their reveal

    The Aftermath

    People were quickly to call out the development team of Pixelmon, for scamming them and demanding their money back, of course that did not happen, in Twitter the Pixelmons NFts were going trending because of how terrible they were, and everyone were making memes and making fun of the team.

    People complaining on the pixelmon reveal on Twitter

    They had given out terrible designs, and they never credited the artists that created some of the Pixelmons. People started looking up who is behind the creation of this project and quickly found out the founder. His name was Martin Van Blerk, and 21 year old with 0 experience in developing games, he revealed himself on the Pixelmon Discord channel because people were looking into him. There he made a post about himself and that the team will hire professionals to upgrade and fix the disaster that they created. No other updates have happened till that incident and people are waiting for news regarding the project.

    Martin Van Blerk apologizing and introducing himself
